“Raspberries” Creative company website

The site was created for a group of people involved in creativity, engineering and web development.

The site is a simple block system with an obvious separation of elements. This reflects the conciseness and ergonomics of the decisions made by the "Raspberries" group.

In this project, I was involved in both development of graphic design and code.

Particular attention was paid to the adaptability of the website, which was implemented using media queries.
