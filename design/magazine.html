Intentions magazine

In 2021, the EESTEK student organization held elections for the position of treasurer.

The INTENTION magazine was created as part of the election campaign of Alexander "Sausej" Kireichev.

The magazine tells about the development of Alexander as a political figure and as a person with a subtle sense of humor and creativity deserving many awards.

In addition to the main theme, the magazine touches on an overview of the life of other members of the Malinka creative group. of the Malinka creative group.
