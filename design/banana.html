Figure 1

A meme that has become a clothing line and a recognizable brand.

The story began with hunger and the old flatbed scanner Сanon СanoScan. I just decided to scan all the things that were on my desk.

Then I got hungry and before eating the banana (that was nearby) I decided to scan it too.

After various manipulations in the smartphone graphic editor, I left only the most recognizable details of the banana, calling this picture "Figure 1. Just a banana (We ate it then) ".
