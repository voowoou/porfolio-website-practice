Zhuhliy Hrustalic mashup cover

The mashup "Береги себя моя, двухэтажка" (Take care of yourself, my two-story building) created with the participation of the musical group Zhuhliy Hrustalic, received an original illustration, which became the cover of the album.

The cover is based on a poster for Yuri Bykov's film "Fool" with an additional collage of the brutal landscape of post-Soviet bedroom community.
